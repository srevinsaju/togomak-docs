## What is togomak?

Togomak is your DevOps Swiss Army knife, simplifying the complex world of Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) with the power of HCL (HashiCorp Configuration Language). Imagine a world where orchestrating release pipelines, building, and deploying your applications seamlessly becomes as effortless as flipping a switch.

Togomak acts as your CI/CD maestro, harmonizing diverse CI/CD systems like GitHub Actions, Jenkins, and Cloud Build, all through the intuitive and powerful HCL. Unlike traditional YAML-based configurations, HCL's elegance and expressiveness allow you to define your pipelines with clarity and precision.

Transform your local development environment into a playground, where you can test and iterate on fixes without waiting for eternity on CI/CD pipelines.

Say goodbye to pipeline chaos and hello to unified CI/CD bliss with Togomak. It's the conductor that orchestrates your software's journey, making sure it sounds harmonious across all environments.
